About ABES Engineering College

ABES Engineering College (ABES EC), Ghaziabad, is one of the top engineering institutions in Uttar Pradesh. Established in 2000, ABES EC has been providing world-class education and producing skilled professionals in the field of engineering, technology, and management. The campus is known for its excellent infrastructure, advanced laboratories, and a vibrant student community.

With strong industry connections and a focus on innovation, ABES EC offers a holistic learning experience that prepares students for the challenges of the modern workforce. Our vision is to nurture talent and foster a culture of research and entrepreneurship among our students.
