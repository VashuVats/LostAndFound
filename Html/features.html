Features of the Lost & Found Platform

Problem Statement

Many students and faculty members lose valuable personal items within the college premises, ranging from mobile phones and headphones to keys and books. There is no centralized system to report or track these lost and found items efficiently, leading to frustration and loss of property.

Solution Overview

Our Lost & Found platform provides a seamless, user-friendly solution to report lost items and track found objects across the campus. The platform includes:

  • Lost Item Form: A detailed HTML form to submit reports of lost items, including item descriptions, location, and date.
  • Item Listing Page: A dynamic listing of all lost and found items with a search and filter functionality. Users can filter by “Lost Items” or “Found Items” to narrow down results.
  • Image Upload: Option to upload photos of the lost or found item, making it easier for others to identify.
  • Real-time Updates: The platform ensures that the lost and found listings are updated in real-time for quick responses.
